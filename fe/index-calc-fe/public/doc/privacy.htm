4. Cookies and other identifiers

We use cookies and similar tools to enhance user experience, provide services, and understand and improve the way customers use our services. We use cookies to help our system recognize your browser or device and provide you with X-TRADE services.

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that are sent to the computer's hard drive through a web browser for record keeping. Some cookies make it easier for you to navigate our platform, while others are used to enable faster login processes or track your activities on our platform. There are two types of cookies: session cookies and permanent cookies.

Session Cookies exist only during online sessions. When you close your browser or turn off your computer, it disappears from your computer. We use session cookies to allow our system to uniquely identify you during a session or while you log in to the platform. This allows you to log in and process your online transactions and requests as you move through our platform and verify your identity.

Permanent cookies remain on the computer even after closing the browser or turning off the computer. We use persistent cookies to track aggregation and statistical information about user activities. Our system is currently not responding to a no-tracking signal.

Disabling cookies allows most web browsers to automatically allow cookies, but you can block cookies later by editing browser options if you want. In most browsers, general settings tell you how to disable new cookies on your computer, how to notify your browser when you receive new cookies, or how to disable them completely. Platform users who have disabled cookies cannot browse specific areas of the platform.

Third-party analysis evaluates the use of our platform using automated devices and applications such as our Google Analysis. We can also use other means of analysis to evaluate our platform. We use these tools to improve our platform, performance, and user experience. These entities can use cookies and other tracking techniques to perform services. We do not share your personal information with these third parties.

The following are the services provided using our cookies and similar tools.
